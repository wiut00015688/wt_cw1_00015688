Who we?

Our company is the official representative of prestigious public universities in the UK, USA, Australia and Holland. Our highly qualified specialists also help students to enter the ranking universities in Canada, New Zealand, UAE, Germany, Singapore and Switzerland. Thanks to high professionalism and a modern approach, over the 8 years of our activity, we have assisted more than 3,000 students to realize their dream! World.uz clients have become students of the most elite universities in the world.

Our mission

We, the World.uz team, believe that in the modern world everyone should have the opportunity to get a quality education. Our mission is to make the world a better place, starting with education, because high-quality education is the foundation of a full and successful life. The main principle of our company is the quality of work performed and an individual approach to each client.
